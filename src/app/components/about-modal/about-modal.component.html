About NMTV

Your Personal Music Television Experience

NMTV is a tribute to the golden era of music television, recreating the experience of watching MTV in its heyday. This app lets you curate your own music video channels, mixing official playlists with your personal favorites, complete with bumpers and that authentic broadcast feel.

Built with modern web technologies, NMTV brings back the joy of discovering music videos without algorithms or interruptions - just pure music television the way it was meant to be.

Switch between decades, explore different genres, and enjoy an endless stream of music videos from YouTube, all presented in that classic MTV style we all remember and love.
