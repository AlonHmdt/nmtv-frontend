Introduction

Welcome to NMTV - Noa's Music Television ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android TV application handles data and respects your privacy.

Information We Do NOT Collect

NMTV does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. We do not:

  • Collect your name, email, or contact information
  • Track your location
  • Access your device's camera or microphone
  • Collect payment information
  • Share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Use analytics or tracking services

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores minimal data locally on your Android TV device for functionality purposes only:

  • Last Selected Channel: We remember which music channel you last watched to provide a seamless experience when you reopen the app.
  • Custom Playlist Preferences: If you add custom YouTube playlists, these preferences are stored locally on your device (maximum 5 playlists per channel).

This data never leaves your device and is only used to enhance your viewing experience.

Third-Party Services

YouTube API

NMTV uses the YouTube Data API v3 to fetch and display music videos. When you use the app:

  • Video content is streamed directly from YouTube's servers
  • YouTube's own terms of service and privacy policy apply to the video content
  • We do not control YouTube's data collection practices
  • Please review YouTube's Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Internet Permission

The App requires internet access to:

  • Stream music videos from YouTube
  • Load channel playlists
  • Fetch video metadata (titles, artists)

We do not use this permission to collect or transmit your personal information.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data at all, the app is safe for all ages. However, YouTube content is subject to YouTube's own age restrictions and content policies.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal security risk. All data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's own security features.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no data to request, modify, or delete from our servers. You can clear the app's local data at any time through your Android TV settings:

  • Go to Settings → Apps → NMTV → Clear Data
